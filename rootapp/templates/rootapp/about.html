Smart Topic Extractor Web Application

Smart Topic Extractor is a basic and essential subject for text mining and information retrieval. It is content analysis method that automatically extracts the most used and most valuable words and phrases from a text. It helps to summarize the matter of texts and identify the main topics considered. Topic detection, topic modelling, or topic extraction is a machine learning method that combines and understands huge sets of text data by generating “tags” or classifications according to specific topic or theme. When we need to analyze huge amounts of text data, is a huge task to do it manually because it is time-consuming. Manually categorization through large volume of data creates more troubles and mistakes. New advancement had made an easy way for achievements in extraction method and deep learning in text mining.
